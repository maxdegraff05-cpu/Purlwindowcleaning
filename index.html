Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I have my windows cleaned?

For most homes, we recommend professional cleaning 2-4 times per year. Commercial buildings may need more frequent cleaning depending on location and exposure to elements.

Do I need to be home for the service?

No, you're not required to be home. We just need access to your property. Many of our clients provide a gate code or hide a key for us.

What happens if it rains after my window cleaning?

We offer a 48-hour rain guarantee. If it rains within 48 hours of our service, we'll come back and re-clean your windows at no extra charge.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We're also certified for high-rise window cleaning work.
